Emperor #1: Caesar Augustus
Full name: Imperator Caesar Augustus
Lived: 63 BC - AD 14
Reigned: 27 BC - AD 14   (40 years)
How he died: natural causes / old age
Interesting Bits:
  • First emperor
  • Ended Roman civil war when he defeated his rival Marcus Antony and his ally and lover Cleopatra
  • Funded numerous major building projects around Rome
